Our Values

Faith-Based

Rooted in our faith, we embrace the teachings of Christ to foster holistic development, character and compassion. Through our programs, we aim to make a positive difference in the lives of youth, promoting grace and hope.

Our Values

Empathy and Respect

We value the uniqueness of every individual, treating all with compassion and dignity. By fostering understanding and embracing differences, we create a safe and inclusive space where personal growth and collective learning thrive.

Our Values

Community Engagement

We actively engage with the community to address the needs of the youth and contribute positively to society.

Our Values

Youth Empowerment

Youth empowerment is at the heart of Move Malawi. We strive to equip youth with the tools, resources, and opportunities they need to reach their full potential. Through mentorship and formal and informal education, we foster positive change.

Our Values

Equality

We believe in providing equal opportunities or all, regardless of their background, gender, race or socioeconomic status. We strive to create an inclusive environment where every individual is valued, respected and given a fair chance to succeed.
