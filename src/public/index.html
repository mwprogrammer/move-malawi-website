A Brief History of Move Malawi

In 2020, a group of young and passionate individuals came together to address some of the challenges brought on by the pandemic. This collaboration led to the birth of an organization aimed at supporting fellow youth - Move Malawi .

Since May 2020, we've grown and learnt to advocate for youth education in our community. Through various projects, we empower young individuals with the knowledge, skills and opportunities they need to succeed.

In just 3 years, we have witnessed success stories that encourage us to continue building a brighter future for the youth in our community. Join us in creating a future where every youth has the opportunity to thrive and make a lasting impact.
